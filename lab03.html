What I Ate for Breakfast

My breakfast is typically a ritual. I eat the same things in the morning every day. I wake up, go to FoCo, and get my classic scrambled eggs and oatmeal. I put hot sauce on my eggs and brown sugar in my oatmeal, and it is awesome. I sit down on Lightside and listen to the FoCo music bump. Then, I am on my way to whatever it is I have going on that day. However, before I leave, I always take a muffin (or whatever delectable baked good is available) for the road.

What I Ate for Lunch

For lunchtime, I am usually pretty hungry. This is because I have class before lunch always, which is somewhat a rule for me. FoCo, especially in the summer, is usually pretty terrible for lunch. This means I usually have the special if it is edible, or I get a smoked turkey sub from the deli section, with just a dash of honey mustard. I usually will couple this with a banana or another piece of fruit. Yum!
